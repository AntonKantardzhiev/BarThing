About Cheerzz


A company founded in Antwerp City providing delicious cocktails for everyone at home!

When Covid-19 hit our lives we decided to make a change for peoples social lives. We provide cocktails delivered at your doorstep to make whenever you want. Not only because of Covid have we decided to make lives easier drinking wise, we also find that this is the future.
No more relying on night shops to get alcohol or drinking until the end at cafés. Now you can enjoy cocktails at your home 24/7!
